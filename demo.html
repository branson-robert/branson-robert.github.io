JavaScript Events

Left click Jonas (do not hold) and he will chase your mouse around the screen.

Right click (do not hold) and move your mouse and he will return to his original position.

Touch Jonas (do not hold) and move your finger around the screen and he will chase your finger or tap the screen in different spots and he will move there.

To reset: touch and hold, let go, and then touch anywhere on the screen or simply reload the page.
